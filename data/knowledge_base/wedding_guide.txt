# Wedding Planning Timeline (India)

## 16 Weeks Before Event
- Finalize wedding date
- Book venue (critical — venues in Bangalore fill 6+ months early in peak season)
- Set overall budget

## 12 Weeks Before Event
- Book caterer
- Shortlist photographers
- Decide decor theme

## 8 Weeks Before Event
- Finalize guest list
- Send invitations
- Book makeup artists

## 4 Weeks Before Event
- Confirm vendor bookings
- Plan logistics and transport
- Trial runs for catering and decor

## 2 Weeks Before Event
- Final confirmations with all vendors
- Prepare contingency plans

---

# Catering Pricing (India)

- Bangalore catering cost:
  ₹900–₹1200 per plate (standard wedding)
- Premium catering:
  ₹1200–₹2000 per plate
- Corporate events:
  ₹600–₹900 per plate

---

# Venue Pricing Trends

- Bangalore:
  ₹2L – ₹10L depending on capacity and location
- Peak season (Oct–Feb):
  Prices increase by 20–30%
- Off-season:
  Discounts up to 25%

---

# Vendor Booking Order

Correct order:
1. Venue
2. Caterer
3. Decor
4. Photographer
5. Entertainment

Incorrect order leads to conflicts.

---

# Negotiation Tips

- Always negotiate catering per plate price
- Ask for bundled discounts (venue + decor)
- Advance payments should not exceed 30–40%
- Compare at least 3 vendors before finalizing

---

# Outdoor Wedding Considerations

- Weather risk in Bangalore (unexpected rain)
- Backup indoor venue required
- Additional lighting and sound costs

---

# Common Mistakes

- Booking vendors before venue
- Underestimating guest count
- Ignoring contingency budget (minimum 10%)
